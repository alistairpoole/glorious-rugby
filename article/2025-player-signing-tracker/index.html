Old Glory DC 2025 Player Signing Tracker

By Alistair Kirsch-Poole
Dec 1, 2024 - 5:00pm

Old Glory DC has announced that they will be revealing their 2025 roster over the coming month, making this a good time to reboot the player signings tracker. We already know from prior comments by head coach Simon Cross that most of the team will be returning, but there will be some changes coming down the pike. Major League Rugby teams are limited to a roster of 39 players, which was the size of the squad last year, meaning that with new players confirmed coming in through signings and the draft, there will have to be players leaving to make space for them. I'll keep things update here as announcements are made.

Note: players marked as "Rumored" have been reported by other news organizations as returning or leaving, but have not been confirmed by the team or the players themselves.
