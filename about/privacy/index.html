Privacy Policy

This site does collect a small amount of anonymous statistics using GoatCounter, a privacy-centric, open-source product that intentionally avoids collecting PII. These statistics are used to understand what content is being accessed and how, so taht we can determine what content connects with readers and ensure compatibility with user devices. Information collected includes the device type, screen size, browser, country/region, and referral information. You can read more about how GoatCounter processes user data in their privacy policy.

This site is also served through Cloudflare, an internet backbone company that manages traffic to the site. Cloudflare does not store PII for the services that this site uses, but may use cookies for the purposes of managing traffic flow and load balancing. You can read more about how Cloudflare uses cookies in their privacy policy.
