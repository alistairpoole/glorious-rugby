Old Glory DC 2026 Player Signing Tracker

By Alistair Kirsch-Poole
Oct 23, 2025 - 12:00pm

Note: This article will be updated as signings are made. Check back here for the latest! Last updated on October 24, 2025.

The turmoil in the offseason has (hopefully) come to close, and teams have begun announcing players returning to their rosters for the 2026 season. This goes for Old Glory as well, who have already announced the return of Cory Daniel as well the departure of several players.

There will be signficant changes, with the foreign player cap shrinking from 10 spots on matchday 23 to just 7, with Canadians now counting against that cap as well. While Old Glory's roster was already heavily American, these new restrictions will have an impact.

Note: players marked as "Rumored" have been reported by other news organizations as returning or leaving, but this status has not been confirmed by the team or the players themselves. Players marked as "Questionable" are not guaranteed to be leaving, but have circumstances that make it unlikely.
