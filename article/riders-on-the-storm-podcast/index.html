Riders on the Storm (podcast)

By Alistair Kirsch-Poole
Jun 28, 2023 - 5:50pm

Old Glory has done it! In the team's first appearance in the playoffs, they took down New York in an instant classic, upsetting the Ironworkers on the road. Alistair shares his experiences traveling to the match on the supporters bus, then we talk about how the game went. And, of course, we preview the upcoming match, the conference finals against New England.
