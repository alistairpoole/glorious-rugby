Soon, the season starts for real (podcast)

By Alistair Kirsch-Poole
Feb 15, 2023 - 4:00pm

It's here! With preseason wrapped up and in the bag, the start of the 2023 season is just around the corner. In just a few days, we will be watching MLR yet again in all its glory.

In this episode of the Glorious Rugby podcast, Fitzy and I take a look back at the second preseason match against Toronto and give our thoughts on how preseason went generally. Then we dive into the week one opponent, the Chicago Hounds, and share some predictions for how the match will go.
