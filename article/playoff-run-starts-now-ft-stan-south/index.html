Playoff Run Starts Now (ft. Stan South)

By Alistair Kirsch-Poole
May 2, 2023 - 12:09pm

Stan South joins us to talk about how Old Glory has changed from last year, the culture of the team, how his role has changed since his season-ending injury, and the dark arts of the scrum. We also talk about what we want to see from Old Glory in this final stretch, with the playoffs a very real possibility.
